Video tips

Recall you may view the videos as often as you wish. To get the most from them, try these tips

  • Use pause to momentarily stop the video
  • Rewind to repeat passages
  • Turn on captions with CC in lower right
  • Change settings to slow down or speed up video

Other tips

Working with lectures and exercises

  • If you wish to make notes on the lecture PDF,
    • open the PDF in a separate browser window
    • annotate the PDF while watching the video
  • Before performing a computer exercise
    • read the exercise instruction PDF
    • listen to the exercise description
    • have the exercise instruction PDF open while conducting analyses with Distance
